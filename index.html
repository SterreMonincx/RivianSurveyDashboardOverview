Maturity Classification Methodology

Note: Maturity levels are derived from analysis of responses to: "In two sentences, how would you describe your current position in implementing and transforming towards a generative AI-enabled organization?"
Maturity Level Classification Criteria Example Keywords/Phrases
Early Stage
 Minimal or no organized implementation "Hardly done anything", "individual basis", "advanced Google search"
Exploring
 Analyzing opportunities, defining strategy, planning phase "Fundamental opportunity", "analyzing", "scan", "initial", "roadmap", "open-minded"
Piloting
 Active implementation, testing, executing specific projects "Work in progress", "implementing", "executing", "process in place", "made a start"
Scaling
 Strategic approach, systematic rollout, client-facing implementation "Strategic approach", "center of excellence", "leveraging", "actively implementing"
Advanced
 Organizational transformation, embedded AI capabilities, cultural shift "Actively evolving", "embedding AI capabilities", "internal AI platform", "cultural shift"
Methodology: Classifications were derived by analyzing keywords and phrases in survey responses, content depth, and stated organizational approach to GenAI implementation.
